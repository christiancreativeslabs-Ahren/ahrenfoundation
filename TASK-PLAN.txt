AHREN FOUNDATION – MENTOR GUIDELINES
Thank you for answering the call to shape a generation.
OUR VISION
A global network of believers in tech using their skills and creativity to build valuable products and services — creating value, impacting lives, and revealing Jesus Christ.

YOUR ROLE AS A MENTOR
You are not a technical teacher. You are not a career coach. You are:
Role
What It Means
Encourager
"You can do this. Your skills, gifts, and creativity have purpose."
Question‑Asker
"What did you learn this week? What was hard?"
Story‑Teller
Share your wins and failures — be real.
Prayer Partner
Pray with and for your mentee.
Door‑Opener
Introduce them to personal & professional development platforms/opportunities, introduce them to someone in your network (when appropriate).
Accountability Partner
"Did you do what you said you would do?"

THE 9‑WEEK MENTORSHIP STRUCTURE
Month
Weekly Emails (Mentee receives)
Monthly Virtual Session (You lead)
Month 1
Weeks 1–3
Session 1: Purpose & Holy Spirit
Month 2
Weeks 4–6
Session 2: Character & Skills
Month 3
Weeks 7–9
Session 3: Project & Commissioning

Each monthly session should include:
Check‑in (10 min)
Discuss the month's lessons (20 min)
Help refine mentee's Kingdom project (20 min)
Prayer and next steps (10 min)
WHAT IS A "KINGDOM PROJECT"?
A project built by the mentee that:
Reveals Jesus Christ
Serves people, the local church or missions
Solves a real problem with tech or creativity
Examples: A discipleship app, a church website, a faith‑based social media page, a video series for youth fellowship, a tool for missionaries.
Your job is not to build it for them. Your job is to ask questions, encourage, and help them clarify the vision.

SAFEGUARDING – OUR NON‑NEGOTIABLE RULE
Never be alone with a mentee in a private, unrecorded setting.
Acceptable
Not Acceptable
Zoom / Google Meet (with recording on)
Private WhatsApp video call (unrecorded)
Public space / church hall
Your home or mentee's home (without others present)
Group setting with other mentors/youths
Late‑night private messaging

If you ever feel uncomfortable, report immediately to Ahren leadership. No question is too small.

WHAT TO DO IF...
Situation
Action
Mentee misses a session
Send a gentle reminder. Log as absent.
Mentee shares something concerning (depression, abuse, etc.)
Counsel as led by the Spirit of God. Notify  Ahren leadership within 24 hours.
You cannot continue mentoring
Notify Ahren immediately. We will reassign the mentee.
You have a great idea to organize for a paid course or training for our mentees.
Pitch it to us at the leadership level. Revenue split: 70% you / 30% Ahren.
